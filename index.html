History of Kheri Fort:

The Kheri Fort is a fort situated near Jaipur city in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It was built by Thakur Budh Singh in the 18th century. The fort is approximately 45 kilometres from Jaipur city.

The place consists of a huge garden with lush greenery, consisting of various types of plants. The entrance gate of the fort is a majestic gate made of wood and iron, which is highly aesthetic.

There is also a ramp staircase beautifully built in the middle of the fort which links both the garden area and entrance door of this picturesque fort.

The fort still serves as a residence for a royal family, but some parts of it have been converted into a tourist spot.

The fort is situated at a high and safe place, from which the royal family used to watch over the surrounding area.

The majestic walls of this fort were constructed using various kinds of materials such as mud, dressed stone, mud enclosed within layers of stones, and lime plaster with various artworks of animals engraved on the walls.

The Kheri Fort is a symbol of comfort and heritage. It is one of the heritage properties where you can enjoy vintage jeep rides and horse riding. This is one of the best and most beautiful forts in Rajasthan to stay. One can feel the combination of privilege of royalness and a typical Rajasthani vibe.

At this place, you can explore royal adventure as well as village life because this fort is located at the heart of the village. Please visit and explore this marvelous fort called Kheri fort.
